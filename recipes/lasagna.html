Lasagna

Description

The classic lasagna is an italian dish comprised of different layers of meat, cheese, sauce, vegetables and anything inbetween seperated by sheets of dough.

Usually you will find different types of grated cheese and herbs such as parsley on top of it. Keep in mind that one pan of lasagna feeds a whole lot of people.

Ingredients

For the sauce

  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 lbs ground beef
  • 1/2 medium onion
  • 1/2 large bell pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 ounce of tomato sauce
  • 3 ounces tomato paste
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp chopped / dried oregano
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 1 tbsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp red or white vinegar
  • sugar to taste
  • salt

For the lasagna

  • 9 sheets of lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces of ricotta cheese
  • 1.5 lbs mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 lbs Parmesan cheese

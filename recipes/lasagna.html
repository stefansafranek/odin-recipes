Lasagna

Description

The classic lasagna is an italian dish comprised of different layers of meat, cheese, sauce, vegetables and anything inbetween seperated by sheets of dough.

Usually you will find different types of grated cheese and herbs such as parsley on top of it. Keep in mind that one pan of lasagna feeds a whole lot of people.

Ingredients

For the sauce

  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 lbs ground beef
  • 1/2 medium onion
  • 1/2 large bell pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 ounce of tomato sauce
  • 3 ounces tomato paste
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp chopped / dried oregano
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 1 tbsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp red or white vinegar
  • sugar to taste
  • salt

For the lasagna

  • 9 sheets of lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces of ricotta cheese
  • 1.5 lbs mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 lbs Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil a large pot of water with added salt
  2. I a large pan heat 2 tbsp of olive oil on medium heat. Add the ground beef and cook until brown on all sides. Put the beef in a bowl and drain all but 1 tbsp of fat.
  3. Slice the bell pepper, dice the onion, mince the garlic. Add the pepper and onion in the pan and cook until the onion is translucent and the pepper is softened. Add the garlic and cook for a minute longer. Put the beef back in and stir while reducing the heat for 5 minutes.
  4. Put the beef mixture in a large pot and add everything tomato to the pot.Add parsley, oregano, seasonings to taste. Add some garlic to taste. Sprinkle in the vinegar and stir in sugar, 1 tbsp at a time to taste. Add salt to taste but keep in mind parmesan is also salty so don't overdo it.
  5. Bring the sauce to a simmer and lower the heat to maintain a light simmer. Cook for 15 to 45 minutes and keep stirring. Make sure to scrape the bottom ever so often so you don't burn anything.
  6. Remove from heat
  7. Put the lasagna sheets in the boiling water to cook them al dente. Make sure to stir ever so often so nothing sticks.
  8. Drain the noodles in a collander and put them on a baking sheet lightly covered in olive oil. Flip the noodles around so that they get covered in oil and don't stick to each other
  9. Heat the oven to 375Â°F.
  10. Take a casserole and put the lasagna sheets at the bottom. Put some sauce on it and grate some cheese on top Repeat this step until there is no more sauce and no more noodles.
  11. Grate the last remaining cheese on top and sprinkle any remaining herbs on it.
  12. Cover the casserole in aluminum foil, slightly tented. Put it in the oven for 45 minutes. If you like it crispy you can remove the foil for the last 10 minutes.
  13. Let it cool for 15 minutes unless you want to burn your tounge.
